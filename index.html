Clock Tower Market
Bustling local bazaar filled with spices, crafts, and history. Symbol of Jodhpur's urban growth.
20 mins
Toorji Ka Jhalra (Stepwell)
An architectural marvel featuring intricate sandstone carvings and a submerged pool of water.
20 mins
City Police
The primary "City Police" location in the heart of the old city. A landmark for local navigation.
20 mins
Pachetiya Hills
A popular spot for travelers to witness breathtaking sunrises and sunsets while exploring narrow lanes.
20 mins
Navchowkiya
Ancient neighborhood designed for royal courtiers. Famous for blue-painted homes.
20 mins
